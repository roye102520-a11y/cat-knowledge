# Cat Not Eating? Reasons Your Cat Refuses Food and What to Do  
# 猫不吃饭？拒食原因与应对

## Introduction / 引言

**English**  
A cat that turns away from breakfast is not always “being picky.” **Loss of appetite** is a top urgent search in cat FAQ databases because it can signal pain, fever, nausea, stress, or organ disease. Cats do not reliably “make up calories tomorrow,” and prolonged fasting in an overweight cat can trigger **hepatic lipidosis** (fatty liver)—a time-sensitive emergency in veterinary practice.

This pillar follows SEO patterns: **introduction**, **causes**, **solutions**, **recommended products**, **tips**, **FAQ**. Link shorter FAQ entries to this page.

**中文**  
猫不吃早饭不一定是「挑食」。在猫 FAQ 数据库里，**食欲下降/拒食**长期位居高紧急度搜索，因为它可能是疼痛、发热、恶心、应激或脏器问题的首发信号。猫不会像部分狗那样「明天再补热量」；**肥胖猫长时间禁食**可能诱发**脂肪肝（肝脂沉积）**，临床上常按**时间敏感急症**处理。

## Causes: Why Cats Stop Eating / 原因：猫为什么不吃

**English**  
**Pain and oral disease:** Dental pain, ulcers, or foreign material can make chewing painful. Interest in food then walking away may point to the mouth.  

**Stress and environment:** New pets, travel, noise, or moving the food bowl can suppress appetite.  

**Nausea / GI:** Hairballs, parasites, pancreatitis, IBD can reduce intake—vomiting may or may not be obvious.  

**Systemic illness:** Kidney, liver, infection, diabetes complications, etc.  

**Food issues:** Spoiled food, sudden changes, or dislike—but **do not dismiss** skipped meals without context.  

**Meds / vaccines:** Some drugs cause nausea; sustained loss after procedures should be reported.

**中文**  
**口腔与疼痛：** 牙痛、牙龈炎、口内异物等可让咀嚼痛苦；有兴趣但不吃、单侧咀嚼，需重视口腔检查。  

**应激与环境：** 新宠物、搬家、噪音、食盆位置改变等可抑制食欲。  

**恶心与肠胃：** 毛球、乱吃、寄生虫、胰腺炎、炎性肠病等均可致进食减少；不一定明显呕吐。  

**全身性疾病：** 肾病、肝病、感染、糖尿病并发症等。  

**食物问题：** 变质粮、突然换粮、口味不适——**勿在无背景情况下**把拒食一律当「挑食」。  

**药物与疫苗：** 部分药致恶心；术后/免疫后持续拒食应告知兽医。

## Solutions: A Practical Ladder / 对策：分步处理

**English**  
**Urgency:** If the cat is **kitten, senior, pregnant, diabetic, or ill**, or has **vomiting, lethargy, hiding, pain**, treat as urgent. A healthy adult missing **one meal** with normal energy may be watched briefly—your vet defines “brief.”  

**Basics:** Fresh food, clean bowl, wide shallow dish, quiet feeding area.  

**Warming:** Slightly warmed food increases aroma; broth only if vet-approved.  

**Vet workup:** History, exam, blood work, urinalysis, imaging, dental when indicated. May include **CBC/chemistry**, **kidney markers**, **thyroid in seniors**, **pancreatitis panels**, **ultrasound**, **dental radiographs**. Not every cat needs every test.  

**Kittens vs seniors:** Kittens can become hypoglycemic/dehydrated quickly—**earlier vet contact**. Seniors with sudden half-portions may mask arthritis, kidney, or dental disease—**earlier evaluation saves crises**. Overweight cats: prolonged fasting is higher risk.

**中文**  
**紧急度：** **幼猫、老年、孕猫、糖尿病或慢性病**，或伴**呕吐、萎靡、躲藏、疼痛**，应视为更紧急。健康成猫仅少吃一顿、精神尚可，可短观察——**「短」由兽医结合个体定义**。  

**基础：** 粮是否新鲜、碗是否干净、浅口大碗、安静远离争抢。  

**加温：** 略加热可提高适口性；肉汤等**须兽医同意**再考虑。  

**诊疗：** 病史、体检、血检、尿检、影像、必要时口腔。可能含 **血常规/生化**、**肾指标**、**老年甲功**、**胰指标**、**超声**、**牙片**等——非每猫全做。  

**幼 vs 老：** 幼猫少吃几顿即可低血糖/脱水，**宜早联系兽医**；老年猫突然食量减半可能藏关节/肾/牙问题；**肥胖猫禁食风险更高**。

## Recommended Products / 推荐用品（知识库常见方向）

**English**  
- **Dry foods** in community guides: **鲜朗**, **爱肯拿**, **渴望**, **皇家**, **NOW** (examples vary by region and budget).  
- **Wet food** for hydration and palatability—transition gradually.  
- **Shallow ceramic bowls**; **automatic feeders** (**霍曼**, **小佩**) for timing—**not a cure for medical anorexia**.  
- **Fountains** for hydration.  
- **兽用益生菌**、**营养膏**、**泌尿护理粮** may appear in catalogs—**urinary diets are not a universal appetite fix**.

**中文**  
- **干粮**：社区常讨论 **鲜朗、爱肯拿、渴望、皇家、NOW** 等（地区与预算不同）。  
- **湿粮**：补水与适口性；换粮须渐进。  
- **浅口陶瓷碗**；**自动喂食器（霍曼、小佩）** 便于定时与远程——**不能替代对病理性拒食的诊疗**。  
- **饮水机** 辅助饮水。  
- 库内可有 **兽用益生菌、营养膏、泌尿护理粮**——**泌尿处方/功能粮并非万能开胃**，须遵医嘱。

## Tips / 实用提示

**English**  
Track weight weekly; separate bullied cats at meals; avoid toxic human foods; document timeline for your vet; build internal links to “cat vomiting,” “kidney disease,” “dental disease.”

**中文**  
每周称重；多猫防抢食；勿喂洋葱/葡萄/木糖醇等毒物；给兽医时间线；站内链到 **猫呕吐、肾病、口腔** 等专题。

## FAQ / 常见问题

| EN | 中文 |
|----|------|
| **How long can a cat fast safely?** No single number for every cat; **hepatic lipidosis** risk rises with prolonged fasting. **猫能饿多久？** 无统一安全天数；禁食过久有脂肪肝风险。 |
| **Treats only—okay?** Treats are not a balanced diet. **只吃零食行吗？** 不行，不能替代全价主食。 |
| **Force-feed?** Risk of aspiration; assisted feeding under vet direction. **能硬灌吗？** 易误吸，需兽医指导方案。 |
| **Warming food?** Often helps aroma. **要加热吗？** 多数猫更愿吃温的。 |
